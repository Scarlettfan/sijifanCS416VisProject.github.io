The reason can be explained by the Delta Variant explosion in mid 2021 across the states. According to the data from Johns Hopkins University Database, the number of confirmed cases hit a 15-month low in late June. However, due to the delta variant rapidly spreading across the U.S, it is about 43,700 new cases per day over the past week, which is up 65% over the previous seven days and nearly three times as high as the level two weeks ago.
